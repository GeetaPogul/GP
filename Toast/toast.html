Toast

This component can be used for toast or snackbar component. Toast is mostly used to show feedback message. Snackbar is to used show message that need user action.

Example of Toast


Saved !

Retry in 5 seconds !

Saving to server !


HTML 

 
    
            <div class=" toast-box" > 
                <div class="toast toast-success">
                    Saved ! <br> 
                    <button class="toast-icon" >
                     <i class="fas fa-times" id="snackbar-close-btn" title="dismiss"></i>
                    </button>
                </div> <br>
        
        
                <div class="toast toast-error">
                   Retry in 5 seconds ! <br> 
                    <button class="toast-icon" >
                    
                    <i class="fas fa-times" id="snackbar-close-btn" 
                    title="dismiss"></i>
                    </button>
                </div> <br> 
        
        
                <div class="toast toast-warning">
                    Saving to server ! <br> 
                    <button class="toast-icon" > 
                    <i class="fas fa-times" id="snackbar-close-btn"
                     title="dismiss"></i>
                    </button>
                </div> <br>
        
            </div>
